Web

As a freelancer, I have worked on projects that use my fine art and also my graphic art skills. What separates me from my competition is my broad understanding of physical work as well as web design. I am able to communicate with web developers and the customer to give realistic expectations to both sides. I create websites using code and also website builders, giving me the ability to give the client a visual web design quickly and efficiently, but also create responsive web layouts.

Clean eye catching design, for a modern business.
